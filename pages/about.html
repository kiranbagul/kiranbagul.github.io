Who I Am

I bring strong leadership expertise in building and scaling high-throughput, lean, cross-functional agile teams in fast-paced environments. With 18+ years of experience, I have a proven track record of collaboratively building, growing, and optimizing products while fostering self-organizing teams with a strong culture of ownership.

At Entain, I played a key role in establishing a new tech center in Pune, working closely with leadership to define and execute product and technical roadmaps. I lead the Player Experience domain for the US market, managing 70+ engineers across multiple locations, driving tech modernization and transformation efforts for Sportsbook and Betting engine product suites.

Previously, at Bank Jago (Dkatalis Labs), I led the ground-up development of core banking and bill payment systems, scaling the engineering team to 40 members while championing engineering best practices. I've also worked with various product organizations as a consultant, building scalable and user-centric products.

I'm passionate about distributed systems, platform engineering, agile transformations, and cloud infrastructure. I thrive on solving complex engineering challenges and fostering a culture of ownership and excellence.

Beyond work, I enjoy poetry, trekking, and science outreach activities. I have a keen interest in astronomy and geology, and built SkyWiki—an Android app for amateur astronomers with 100K+ downloads. I believe in sharing knowledge and contributing to the community through tools and apps that have reached hundreds of thousands of users worldwide.
